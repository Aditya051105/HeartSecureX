What is HeartSecureX?

HeartSecureX is an intelligent heart disease prediction system designed to empower individuals and healthcare providers with early risk insights. By analyzing vital parameters and lifestyle factors, it provides a personalized probability of heart disease risk.

Understanding Heart Diseases

Heart diseases remain the leading cause of death globally. Early detection and management are crucial to preventing severe complications.

  • Coronary artery disease: Blocked heart arteries.
  • Heart attack: Interrupted blood flow to heart muscles.
  • Arrhythmia: Irregular heart rhythms.
  • Heart failure: Reduced pumping ability.
